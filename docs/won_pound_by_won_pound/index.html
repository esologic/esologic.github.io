Won Pound by Won Pound (April of 2022)

Artwork and music video for Won Pound album.

Client wanted accompanying visuals for an album themeatically focused on self-reflection. Worked with client to capture over 50,000 images to then train a StyleGAN network. Then, created GANce framework to input music and create animations. Won Pound by Won Pound was released via Minaret Records.
